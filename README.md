# Retail Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzed retail sales data to identify trends in product categories, customer demographics, and buying behaviour. Created pivot tables and charts to make insights easy to understand and actionable.

## Data Description
The dataset contains retail sales information with the following columns:

- **customer_id** – unique identifier for each customer
- **gender** – Male/Female
- **age** – customer age
- **prod_category** – category of the product purchased
- **quantity** – number of units purchased
- **per_unit_price** – price per unit
- **total_amount** – quantity × per_unit_price
- **Month** – month of purchase
- **Month number** – for sorting months chronologically
- **Age group** – bucketed age ranges (e.g., 18-25, 26-35, etc.)

## Analysis Steps
1. **Sales by Gender**
   - Pivot table to calculate total sales by gender
   - Visualized in a chart to show revenue distribution

2. **Revenue by Product Category**
   - Pivot table to sum total_amount by product category
   - Identified top-selling categories

3. **Sales by Age Group**
   - Pivot table to sum total_amount by age group
   - Highlighted key customer segments

4. **Sales by Month**
   - Pivot table to sum total_amount by month
   - Line chart shows sales trends over the year

## Key Insights
- Gender distribution in sales  
- Top revenue-generating product categories  
- Age group with highest purchasing power  
- Seasonal trends in sales  

## How to Use
